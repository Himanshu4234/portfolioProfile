## Overview

**LiveLaw** is a premier legal news portal in India, providing real-time updates, court judgments, opinion pieces, and legal analysis.  
The platform is widely trusted by legal professionals, law students, journalists, and anyone following India’s legal landscape.

The site also offers a **premium subscription model**, giving users access to ad-free content, archives, downloadable judgments, and exclusive editorial insights.

---

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Legal Reporting**  
  Covers breaking court news, Supreme Court & High Court judgments, and legal commentary.

- **Subscription-Based Premium Content**  
  Provides paid users with ad-free browsing, unlimited archives, downloadable judgments, and exclusive editorial content.

- **Structured Legal Articles**  
  Offers in-depth analysis, explainers, and opinion pieces on major legal developments.

- **SEO & Semantic Content**  
  Optimized for search engines with structured metadata and rich legal content.

- **Accessibility & Performance**  
  Designed to handle high traffic, large text-heavy pages, and fast-loading content.

- **Mobile App Ecosystem**  
  Available on iOS and Android with bookmarking, notifications, and judgment downloads.

## Challenges & Learnings

- **Performance Optimization** for article-heavy sections and archives.  
- **Designing a Subscription Flow** without disrupting public content users.  
- **Consistent Content Structure** for articles, judgments, and editorials.  
- **SEO for Legal Articles** to rank highly in search results.  
- **Handling High Traffic Peaks** during major court news updates.
